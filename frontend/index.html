Free Educational Resources & Study Materials For All

Sajhi Shiksha is committed to making quality education accessible to every single student and teacher across India. By providing unrestricted, direct access to a wide library of study materials, reference notes, and school worksheets, we aim to support classroom learning, lesson planning, and self-study journeys for multiple school boards including KVS (Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan), CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), and national and state boards.

Our open platform operates under the core belief that financial constraints or technical hurdles should never get in the way of learning. Whether you are a class 6 pupil practicing basic arithmetic, a class 12 scholar preparing for terminal board exams, or a dedicated teacher compiling administrative templates, Sajhi Shiksha offers immediate download links with zero registration walls.

Empowering India's Education Ecosystem

Sajhi Shiksha is an open-access, community-centric repository built with a singular, powerful vision: to make premium educational resources, reference tools, and academic aids entirely open and available to every student and teacher in India. We believe that financial or technical barriers should never prevent a student from practicing mathematics or a teacher from downloading high-quality administrative formats.

Our platform offers direct, login-free downloads of curated study materials, CBSE syllabus resources, and chapter-wise study notes. By removing the need for registrations, we ensure that pupils from Classes 6 to 12 can safely and immediately access the academic material they need to excel, while educators can discover time-saving templates and teaching guidelines.

Shared Knowledge, Shared Growth

The spirit of Sajhi Shiksha centers around "Sharing Knowledge — From You, For You." This means the notes and sheets you find here are not generic web scrapes; they are shared directly by dedicated teachers, TGT and PGT subject matter experts, and brilliant students who want to make learning a cooperative journey. From exam preparation booklets to Olympiad worksheets, the database keeps expanding.

Whether you are a student preparing for board exams, a parent looking for practice worksheets, or a teacher looking to share your worksheets and rules formats, this platform is open to all. By distributing useful and structured files, we are building a national study pool that empowers government school systems (like Kendriya Vidyalaya), private CBSE academies, and rural state-board schools alike.
